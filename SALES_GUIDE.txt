SALES TIPS
----------
1. Show, Don't Tell
   - Create demo websites with local business names
   - Show on your phone to demonstrate mobile responsiveness
   - Have a portfolio ready

2. Focus on Benefits
   - More customers through better SEO
   - Professional image builds trust
   - Mobile users can easily contact them
   - 24/7 online presence

3. Overcome Objections
   "It's too expensive" → Compare to custom development ($5k+)
   "I can use Wix/Squarespace" → Show professional difference
   "I don't have time" → You handle everything
   "I already have a website" → Show improvement potential

4. Create Urgency
   - Limited spots available
   - Special launch pricing
   - Competitors are getting ahead
   - First month free hosting

5. Offer Guarantees
   - 100% satisfaction guarantee
   - Unlimited revisions
   - 30-day support included
   - Money-back if not satisfied
