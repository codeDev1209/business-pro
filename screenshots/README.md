## How to take screenshots:

### Windows:
- Press `Win + Shift + S` for region capture
- Or use `Print Screen` then paste in Paint

### Mac:
- Press `Cmd + Shift + 4` for region capture
- Or `Cmd + Shift + 3` for full screen
